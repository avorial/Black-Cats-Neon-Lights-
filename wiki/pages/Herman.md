# Herman

## Overview

Herman is an artist who begins producing cartoons for Ariel's paper. His downtime establishes a mundane income source, a firearm purchase, and an emerging method for intentionally producing prophetic illustrations.

## Downtime Timeline

### 2021-10-25

#### Summary
Herman started drawing cartoons for the paper connected to [[Ariel Fontaine]], earning a minor income and improving his French through cartoon balloons. He bought a Chamelot-Delvigne Model 1873 revolver with ammunition. He also tried to intentionally connect with the force or person on the other side that causes his prophetic illustrations.

#### Clues and Discoveries
- Herman's cartoons sometimes show the future when drawn in his sleep.
- He caught himself drawing a lantern with crystal-focused light shining on a strapped-down man.
- The prophetic cartoons are made at 11:30 at night.
- If Herman is awake at 11:30, nothing is made; if asleep, there is a chance of a drawing.
- This is the first clue that he can do it with purpose.
