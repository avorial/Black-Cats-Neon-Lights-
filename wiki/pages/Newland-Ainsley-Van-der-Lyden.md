## Character Overview

**Name:** Newland Ainsley Van Der Lyden

Born into a wealthy California family of wine producers, Newland joined the American Reserve Officer Corps to satisfy his parents' expectations. After completing his service, he found himself increasingly drawn toward forgotten lore, occult mysteries, and forbidden knowledge. Rather than pursuing another respectable career, he began expressing these unsettling fascinations through poetry, seeking truth in places most people refuse to look. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

## That Deuced Peculiar Business

Ever since your military service, your nights have belonged to another man.

He is someone you killed in the line of duty.

He never accuses you.

He never threatens you.

Instead, he simply whispers.

Each dream brings strange phrases in languages you have never studied, fragments of occult knowledge that somehow linger in your memory after waking. The words feel ancient, dangerous, and impossibly meaningful.

You cannot decide whether they are the product of guilt...

...or whether the dead have truly found a way to speak.
