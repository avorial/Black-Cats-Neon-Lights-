# From the Sun Comes Shadows

## Overview

An investigation note involving [[Le Soleil d'Or]], [[Lucien Valadin]], [[Pauline]], and a commission delivered by a Serbian-looking messenger.

## Full Text or Extract

The investigator went to [[Le Soleil d'Or]] to find [[Lucien Valadin]]. [[Pauline]], the owner or keeper of the place, was evasive until told that Lucien was being sought for a commission. Pauline then gave Lucien's address and said Lucien had not come to the bar for weeks, though he normally conducted business there.

The last time Lucien came in, he met a Serbian-looking man who said he was the messenger for Lucien's latest commission. The man handed over a satchel with sketches and photos. Lucien told Pauline the setup was very odd and that, if the price had not been excessive, he likely would not have entertained such distance from his client. That was the last time Lucien was seen at Le Soleil d'Or.
