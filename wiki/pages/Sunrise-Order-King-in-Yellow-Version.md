# Sunrise Order King in Yellow Version

## Overview

A King in Yellow-like text attributed to visions recorded by clergy connected to the heretical [[Sunrise Order]].

## Full Text or Extract

A Greek Orthodox monk told Newland that church legends describe a heretical sect called the Sunrise Order. They believed Jesus was imprisoned by God because mankind was not ready for salvation. The Sunrise Order performed rites to connect to Jesus in the "prison of God." Through visions, one of the clergy wrote down a version of what one would consider The King in Yellow. The sect believed deep understanding of it could reveal a way to free him and let him bring the whole world into his splendor.
