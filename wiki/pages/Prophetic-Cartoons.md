# Prophetic Cartoons

## Overview

Herman's sleep-drawn cartoons that can show future events.

## Appears In

- [[Herman]] - 2021-10-25

## Connected NPCs

- [[Ariel Fontaine]]

## Important Events

- Herman caught himself producing one of the drawings and learned they happen at 11:30 at night.
- If he is awake at that time, nothing is made; if asleep, there is a chance.

## Notes

- The source says this is the first clue that Herman can do it with purpose.
