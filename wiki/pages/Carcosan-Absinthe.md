# Carcosan Absinthe

## Overview

An absinthe that enhances perception of Carcosan magic and gives dangerous insights, visions, or experiences.

## Appears In

- [[Newland Van Der Lynden]] - 2021-10-11
- [[Newland Van Der Lynden]] - 2021-10-25
- [[Newland Van Der Lynden]] - 2021-12-13

## Connected NPCs

- [[Burk Vinet]]
- [[Chloe]]

## Important Events

- Newland used it to view the Master's chamber and saw coffin-to-mirror travel.
- Newland used it with the [[Golden Mirror]] and experienced life as a Walker in [[Carcosa]].
- Newland used it with crystal shards and gained the Corsica/Carcosa insight.

## Notes

- Burk and Chloe refuse it, saying it opens understanding of power but is not a way to use power.
- Some uses require a push or yellow card in the source.
