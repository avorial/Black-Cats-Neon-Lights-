## Character Overview

**Name:** Meredith Katherine "Molly" Cooper

The only child of enormously successful Oklahoma ranchers, Molly was raised with hard work, open skies, and little patience for high society. Her parents, determined to turn their spirited daughter into a respectable young lady, sent her to Paris to study watercolor painting, hoping she would acquire refinement, social grace, and perhaps a wealthy husband.

Molly, however, sees the world as an adventure waiting to be explored.

## That Deuced Peculiar Business

Molly traveled to Paris accompanied by her older brother, **Henry**, who was to serve as her chaperone.

He arranged everything.

Their accommodations.

Their luggage.

Their rooms.

Together they settled into the **Étoile Polaire Apartments** at **20 Rue Théodule Ribot**, known to its residents as **The North Star**.

Then, two weeks later...

Henry was gone.

Not merely missing.

His luggage had vanished.

His room no longer existed.

No one remembered him.

The landlord insisted Molly had always arrived alone. The neighbors stared blankly when she spoke his name. Even letters from home made no mention of a second child.

Most horrifying of all, their parents no longer remembered ever having had a son.

Molly cannot explain what has happened.

But it feels disturbingly familiar, echoing whispered stories of disappearances and forgotten relatives that have haunted the Cooper family for generations.

She is determined to find Henry...

...and unravel whatever ancient curse has begun claiming her family.
