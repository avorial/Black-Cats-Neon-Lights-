# Gold Room Creature

## Overview

A supernatural creature encountered through the [[Villa de Rochas]] Gold Room. The source says it was not created or controlled by [[Albert de Rochas]].

## Appears In

- [[Newland Van Der Lynden]] - 2021-08-09

## Connected NPCs

- [[Albert de Rochas]]

## Important Events

- It fed on energies placed in the Gold Room.
- It crossed into the world when the group came near, similar in principle to the Red Knight crossing near them.

## Notes

- Newland wondered whether gold could kill or affect such monsters because the Gold Room's gold seemed to keep energy in.
