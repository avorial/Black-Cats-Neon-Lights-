## That Deuced Peculiar Business

Whenever you completely lose yourself in your work, something impossible happens.

The sculptures change.

Not dramatically, but subtly, as though someone has altered them while you slept.

More unsettling still are the leaves that gather around your studio.

Each morning they seem to arrange themselves into fragments of verse. Alone they are little more than disconnected sentences, but when assembled they become haunting poems.

The poetry is beautiful.

It is also far beyond anything you could consciously write.

Dark, melancholy, and strangely prophetic, the verses have become your most closely guarded secret.
