# The 7 Lines of the Fountaine Family



## Details
- Paris – Little information is known, the last promoted member of the family Julius, was burned at the stake on August 8 1793.
- Marseille – The Marseille Branch were bankers and merchants; they grew very rich from wise investments. The leader of the branch is Bertrand Fontaine.
- Lyon – The Lyon branch owned textile factories that are the archetype of industrial workhouse.  Adoline Fontaine is the widowed head of the family.
- Toulouse – The Toulouse family is known for lavish parties where they make their money is a question as everyone seems to have a different answer. Art or Underworld practices seem to be the most common tales; Marco Fontaine is the head of this branch.
- Nice – A bastard line, it is believed a house maid of Julius became pregnant and taking some of the wealth left behind in Paris started a home in Nice for her child. They right now make their money in canned fish.
- Strasbourg – The second bastard line, seems to come from Julius brother Charles who’s lover left the Paris to her family's home. They ran an armory that supplied weapons both to the Germans and the French.
- American - This is Ariel's line.
