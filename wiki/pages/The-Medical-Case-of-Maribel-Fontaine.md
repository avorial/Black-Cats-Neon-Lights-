# The Medical Case of Maribel Fontaine

## Overview

A medical handout describing [[Maribel Fontaine]]'s death at [[Hotel-Dieu]], unauthorized surgery by [[Doctor Sasha Kersiki]], organ removal, and an egg found inside her body. The date is inferred from the surrounding Fontaine investigation material; the source handout itself has no explicit date.

## Full Text or Extract

The investigator returned to Hotel-Dieu after eight days and found Maribel apparently asleep in bed, then realized she was dead. Examination showed surgery wounds not present from the original attack. Someone appeared to have removed organs from her.

Hospital staff, cowed by the investigator's authority, said [[Doctor Sasha Kersiki]] came and operated on her. Because the investigator had operated on Maribel rather than a staff doctor, the staff did not feel authorized to stop him. The body was moved to the morgue for autopsy.

The autopsy raised more questions than it answered. Maribel's heart had been removed in a way that damaged her lungs, and it made no sense that she was not cold when found. In the place of the kidney previously removed by the investigator, there was a fist-sized egg. When raised to the light, something could be seen inside it.
