## Character Overview

**Name:** Herman "Art" Wentworth *(Arminius is the Latin form of Herman)*

After graduating from Princeton, Herman joined the **New York Times**, expecting a career writing thoughtful articles and observations. Instead, he was assigned to the newspaper's cartoon desk. Under the tutelage of a veteran cartoonist, Herman discovered an unexpected artistic talent. ## That Deuced Peculiar Business

One evening you fell asleep at your drawing table while working late.

When you awoke, an entire series of cartoons lay before you.

They depicted your cousin Regina being brutally attacked and murdered with a hatchet.

At first you dismissed it as exhaustion.

Then it happened again.

Every few nights you awaken to new drawings, each depicting Regina and strangers you have never met caught in horrifying and impossible events.

You have never consciously drawn any of them.

Without revealing the true reason, you convinced your family to let you travel to Paris under the pretense of pursuing a "real" career.

In truth, you came to discover why your hands continue to create these dreadful visions while you sleep.
