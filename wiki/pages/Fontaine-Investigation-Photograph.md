# Fontaine Investigation Photograph

## Overview

A photograph that changes depending on development method and appears in Ariel's and Molly's investigations.

## Full Text or Extract

Ariel found that different developing methods reveal different images. One version shows the commonly perceived ghost. A second shows a Native American with a bullet hole in his chest. A third shows a Serbian man. Ariel has copies of each alternate photo.

Molly's notes also mention that [[Adele Fontaine]] photographed one Red Knight-related incident before being found dead by [[Jean-Leon Gerome]]. It is uncertain whether these references are to the same photograph or closely related photographic evidence.
