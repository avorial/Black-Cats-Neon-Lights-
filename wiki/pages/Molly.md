### 2021-10-25

#### Summary
Molly investigated [[Jean-Leon Gerome]], concluding he did not personally comport with the [[The Warton Players]]. Evidence suggests he was elsewhere during events attributed to him, while a double or doppelganger appeared as [[The Red Knight]].

#### People

#### Places

#### Documents
- [[Fontaine Investigation Photograph]]

#### Clues and Discoveries
- Gerome has at least seven documented pieces of evidence that he was not at locations he was suspected of attending.
- Gerome seemed aware of a double or doppelganger appearing only as [[The Red Knight]].
- His married paramour, [[Adele Fontaine]], took a photograph of one such event and was later found dead by Gerome.
- It could be suspected that [[Mr. Fontaine]] was in attendance and may have killed Adele, but this is marked as suspicion only.
- Gerome has recurring dreams of Carcosa without knowing the name.
- He has dreamed roughly thirty times of Native Americans connected to Newland's material rending him or the Red Knight to pieces; over time the attackers have dwindled to five men, and the Knight continues to stand.
- The dream changed after the revival of Candida where the first rows of the audience died.
- Molly records a theory that Gerome was strong enough not to participate, so someone or something made a stand-in; Dorian is not as strong and is being consumed by it.

#### Open Threads
