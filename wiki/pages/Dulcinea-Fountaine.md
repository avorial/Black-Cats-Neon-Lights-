## Character Overview

**Name:** Dulcinea "Dee" Fountaine  
**Nationality:** French  
**Background:** Former poor shopkeeper's daughter whose family inherited wealth from a deceased uncle. Despite her newfound fortune, she remains an outsider among Europe's true elite.

## That Deuced Peculiar Business

For months you suffered vivid dreams of sacrifice, exhaustion, and your very will being drained away.

Last week, a terrifying realization struck.

They were never dreams.

A coven of witches had been secretly stealing your life essence, drugging your drinking water so you would sleep through their rituals. Suspecting something was wrong, you secretly replaced your water without alerting them.

When they gathered once more, you remained awake.

As the witches became absorbed in their ritual, you fled into the rain.

Cold, terrified, and unsure who would believe your impossible story, you considered the one place that might offer refuge.

By the time you climbed the steps of the **North Star**, the rain had begun to let up.
