# Golden Mirror

## Overview

A mirror from the trunk that, when examined with [[Carcosan Absinthe]], caused Newland to experience Carcosa as a Walker.

## Appears In

- [[Newland Van Der Lynden]] - 2021-10-25

## Connected NPCs

- [[Burk Vinet]]
- [[Chloe]]

## Important Events

- Newland fell asleep after seeing himself as a Walker.
- He experienced what felt like endless days in [[Carcosa]] while only six hours passed in the real world.
- The current world felt like a dream after waking.

## Notes

- The source does not clarify whether this was travel, possession, vision, or memory transfer.
