# Unidentified Forces by Albert de Rochas

## Overview

A text by [[Albert de Rochas]] about technology, crystals, photography, waves of other-dimensional energy, and supernatural perception.

## Full Text or Extract

Plain reading: The book deals with using technology to sense the supernatural. Crystals have power because waves of other-dimensional energy pass through them. Native cultures and tribal groups once saw these effects, but most knowledge has been lost until the rise of modern technology. Things invisible to human eyes may be visible through other photographic methods.

Intuition reading: The ideas seem to answer questions as they arise, bringing the darkness into the light in a way that feels different from faith-based occult study.

Occult reading: A story describes researchers studying death who learned that light passing through a particular gemstone and shining on a dying person allowed them to see that person's grim reaper, called charos. As long as the light touched it, the person would not die.

Demimonde reading: Rochas has a cult, though they see themselves more as a salon, called [[The Ibroix]]. They meet once every spring and fall. One address is tracked down: [[Rachel Weissman]]. Rochas lives on the outskirts of Paris at [[Villa de Rochas]] and goes out on the 15th of each month.
