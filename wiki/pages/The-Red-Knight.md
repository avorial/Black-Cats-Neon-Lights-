# The Red Knight

## Overview

The Red Knight is a red-clad masked figure, double, stand-in, herald, avatar, or manifestation connected to [[Jean-Leon Gerome]], [[Dorian Van Der Lynden]], prophetic and photographic evidence, and Carcosan dreams.

## Appears In

- [[Ariel Fontaine]] - Date uncertain
- [[Molly]] - 2021-10-25
- [[Newland Van Der Lynden]] - Date uncertain

## Connected NPCs

- [[Jean-Leon Gerome]]
- [[Adele Fontaine]]
- [[Dorian Van Der Lynden]]
- [[Burk Vinet]]

## Important Events

- Gerome seems to have had a double/doppelganger appearing as the Red Knight.
- A Red Knight mask or shapeshifted version impersonated Burk.
- Ariel's occult reading suggests a red masked figure may be a blessing, avatar, herald, empowered costume, or creature crossing into the world.

## Notes

- The source is deliberately uncertain about the Red Knight's nature. This page preserves that uncertainty.
