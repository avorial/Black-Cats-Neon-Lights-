# Carcosa

## Overview

Carcosa is an alien or supernatural land repeatedly encountered through ritual, absinthe visions, mirror travel, crystals, and historical overlap with [[Corsica]].

## Important Events

- Masters can enter and exit supernatural lands such as Carcosa through rituals.
- Newland experiences endless days in Carcosa as a Walker after looking into the [[Golden Mirror]] with absinthe.
- A ritual centuries ago caused an exchange between [[Corsica]] and Carcosa.
- The crystal shards come from Carcosan mines moved into this world.

## Notes

- The source preserves uncertainty around whether experiences in Carcosa are visions, travel, transformation, or some combination.
