# Golden Water Lily

## Overview

An enchanted item that can apparently be empowered by absorbing Carcosan artifacts or cultist/creature energies under specific conditions.

## Appears In

- [[Newland Van Der Lynden]] - 2021-10-25

## Connected NPCs

- [[Burk Vinet]]
- [[Chloe]]

## Important Events

- Burk and Chloe say it can be empowered by destroying Carcosan artifacts into it.
- Another method is to let it rest in the grave of a cultist or creature for a full-moon night until eleven cultist energies are taken.

## Notes

- The source does not say Newland completed either empowerment method before meeting Lepine.
