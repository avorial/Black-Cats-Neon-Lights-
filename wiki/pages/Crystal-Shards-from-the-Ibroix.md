# Crystal Shards from the Ibroix

## Overview

Crystal shards associated with the [[The Ibroix]], [[Albert de Rochas]], and Carcosan mines. Light through them produces a deep yellow spectrum with occult effects.

## Appears In

- [[Newland Van Der Lynden]] - 2021-12-13
- [[Unidentified Forces by Albert de Rochas]]
- [[Herman]] - 2021-10-25, through the crystal lantern vision

## Connected NPCs

- [[Albert de Rochas]]
- [[Rachel Weissman]]

## Important Events

- Light through the shards can reveal or hold a dying creature's spirit-like figure.
- A mouse held in the light could not die by other means but still felt pain and healed normally.
- Absinthe insight says the crystals came from Carcosan mines moved into this world.

## Notes

- The source does not define the full limits of this effect.
