# Albert de Rochas

## Overview

Albert de Rochas is the author of [[Unidentified Forces by Albert de Rochas]], lives on the outskirts of Paris in the [[Villa de Rochas]], and is connected to the [[The Ibroix]] salon/cult. He was once partly trained by [[The Master]] but broke with him over the philosophy of service.
